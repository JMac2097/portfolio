Digital transformation in Asia

Digital transformation is happening across the globe, including in the fast growing Asian market. This master class covers how the Asian market is digitally transforming, how it's using innovation and who the digital leaders are.

3 of 3 Completed
  • Digital transformation in Asia: part 1

    This module is an overview of the rapid and impactful changes transforming Asian businesses as a result of digital technology and consumer behavior. After watching this you will understand the basic principles of how digital has changed the world over the past 15 years and what it means to your business.

  • Digital transformation in Asia: part 2

    In this module we will talk about how technology in APAC has changed the way people connect with family, friends, colleagues and brands as well as how the rapid march of technology & ecommerce has changed the way we buy, browse and discover products,media & services in the region.

  • Digital transformation in Asia: part 3

    In this module we will talk about how technology changes how companies interact with their customers, and how digital culture changes how people do business .

The digital business revolution

Digital impacts today's products and businesses in new and exciting ways - unlocking new potential and changing the way companies can satisfy their customers.

1 of 4 Completed
  • The digital business revolution: Product

    The marketing mix is a tool that is made up of four unique but interconnected and interdependent variables. This module will focus on the product element of the marketing mix. You will see examples of how brands around the world are leveraging the impact of digital on product to engage in opportunities that drive business results.

  • The digital business revolution: Price

    This module will focus on the price element of the marketing mix. You will see examples of how brands around the world are leveraging the impact of digital on price to engage in opportunities that drive results, and how new payment models are changing the way we shop.

  • The digital business revolution: Placement

    This module will focus on the placement element of the marketing mix. You will see examples of how brands around the world are changing the way they go to market and connect with customers, and how today we bring the marketplace to you, where-ever you are, whenever you want it.

  • The digital business revolution: Promotion

    This module will focus on the promotion element of the marketing mix. Previously promotion was often about interruption and reach. In this module you will see examples of how brands around the world are leveraging technology to reach people more effectively to ensure we reach the right person with the right content at the right time, on the right device, in the right location, to drive the right business results.
